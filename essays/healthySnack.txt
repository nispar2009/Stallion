Greek Yogurt Parfait

[[Ingredients]]
1. 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain or flavored)
2. Half cup granola
3. 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
4. 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
5. 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional)

[[Instructions]]
1. [[Layer the Ingredients:]] In a glass or bowl, start by adding a layer of Greek yogurt.
2. [[Add Granola:]] Sprinkle a layer of granola over the yogurt.
3. [[Add Berries:]] Add a layer of mixed berries on top of the granola.
4. [[Repeat Layers:]] Repeat the layers until you reach the top of the glass or bowl.
5. [[Top with Honey and Chia Seeds:]] Drizzle honey on top and sprinkle with chia seeds if desired.
